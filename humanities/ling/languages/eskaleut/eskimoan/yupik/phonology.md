## Segmental

### Vowel

'a', 'i', 'u' 'ə'

Central Yup'ik
```
a, aa, e, ə, i, ii, u, uu
```

(In proximity to the uvular consonants 'q', 'r' or 'rr', the vowel /i/ is pronounced [e], and /u/ is pronounced [o].)
